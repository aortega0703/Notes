For $k\in \mathbb{Z}_n$, $\langle k \rangle=\mathbb{Z}_n\iff \operatorname{gcd}(n,k)=1$.
##### Proof:
$\mathbb{Z}_n = \langle 1 \rangle$ and $k=1^k$, the result then follows from [[Generators of Finite Cyclic Groups]].
