For $s,t\in\mathbb{Z}$ with $t\neq 0$
- If $s=td$ for some $d\in\mathbb{Z}$, then $t$ _divides_ $s$ (denoted $t|s$) and $s$ is a _multiple_ of $t$.
- A _prime_ is an integer $p>1$ whose only positive divisors are $1$ and $p$ itself.
