For $a,b\in\mathbb{Z}$ with $a,b\neq 0$, their _least common multiple_ is the smallest $(d>0)\in\mathbb{Z}$ such that $a|d$ and $b|d$, denoted by $d=\operatorname{lcm}(a,b)$.
