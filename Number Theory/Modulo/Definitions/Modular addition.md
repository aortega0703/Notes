In [[Integers modulo n is a group|$\mathbb{Z}/n$]] modular addition is defined as$$
\begin{align}
	+_n:\hspace{-1.5em}&&\mathbb{Z}/n &\to \mathbb{Z}/n\\
	&&a +_n b &\mapsto a + b\ (\operatorname{mod}\ n)
\end{align}$$
